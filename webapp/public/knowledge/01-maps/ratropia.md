# Ratropia

Ratropia is the main hub of the surface world. Almost every other map (the Hideous Dungeon, Rankle River, the Surface) is reached from one of its outer rooms. This article walks the map starting from **Town Hall** and follows each path outward.

For each room you'll find:

- A short description of what it is
- **Timer:** how long it takes before the room can be triggered again (`TBD` if unknown)
- **Exits:** every connected room and the direction to take

![Ratropia map](images/ratropia-map.png)

Rooms mapped by Bach

## Town Hall (15 min)

The crossroads of Ratropia. Every path radiates from here, so most journeys start (and end) at Town Hall.

- **Timer:** 15 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → Royal Road
  - South → South-shire
  - East → Brown Road
  - West → West-shire
  - Up → Up-shire
  - Down → Down-shire

---

## North path: Royal Road

The north exit climbs into the royal district and the magic academies.

### Royal Road (30 min)

A wide thoroughfare splitting in three: north toward the throne, east toward the Magic Academy, west into the mushroom caves.

- **Timer:** 30 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → North-shire
  - East → Magic Academy
  - West → Shroom Caves
  - South → Town Hall

### North-shire (3 h)

A long corridor between Royal Road and the Royal Gates.

- **Timer:** 3 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Royal Gates
  - South → Royal Road

### Royal Gates (1 h)

Entry point to the royal compound. Branches west toward the Royal Tower, north into the prison wing, east toward the Baths.

- **Timer:** 1 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Royal Prison
  - East → Baths
  - West → Royal Tower
  - South → North-shire

### Royal Tower (2 h)

The tower itself. Connects down to the Training Yard.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - East → Royal Gates
  - South → Training Yard

### Training Yard (2 h)

Dead-end practice ground beneath the Royal Tower.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Royal Tower

### Baths (instant)

A small interlude room east of the Royal Gates that leads to the Royal Prison. Has no regen timer.

- **Timer:** instant (no cooldown)
- **Exits:**
  - West → Royal Gates
  - East → Royal Prison

### Royal Prison (30 min)

End of the prison wing.

- **Timer:** 30 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → Royal Gates
  - West → Baths

### Magic Academy (1 h)

The first room of the magical chain east of Royal Road.

- **Timer:** 1 h
- **Exits:**
  - West → Royal Road
  - East → Scriptorium

### Scriptorium (3 h)

Library between the Magic Academy and the Magic Schools.

- **Timer:** 3 h
- **Exits:**
  - West → Magic Academy
  - East → Magic Schools

### Magic Schools (2 h)

End of the magical chain.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - West → Scriptorium

### Shroom Caves (2 h)

Mushroom caves west of Royal Road. They lead **out of Ratropia to the Surface**. Once on the surface you're on a different map.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - South → Royal Road
  - North → To the Surface (different map)

## South path: South-shire

The south road heads down to the desert hub of Lopsided Lane and the Shifting Sands.

### South-shire (4 h)

Long corridor between Town Hall and Lopsided Lane.

- **Timer:** 4 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Town Hall
  - South → Lopsided Lane

### Lopsided Lane (15 min)

Four-way junction at the heart of the southern lands.

- **Timer:** 15 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → South-shire
  - West → Tor Moor
  - South → Flat Flats
  - East → Shifting Sands

### Tor Moor (2 h)

Marsh west of Lopsided Lane. Drops south to Cliffy Cliffs.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - East → Lopsided Lane
  - South → Cliffy Cliffs

### Cliffy Cliffs (2 h)

Dead-end cliffs south of Tor Moor.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Tor Moor

### Flat Flats (2 h)

Open plains south of Lopsided Lane, leading to the Heist Location.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Lopsided Lane
  - South → Heist Location

### Heist Location (instant)

Special-event room at the southern tip. No regen timer.

- **Timer:** instant (no cooldown)
- **Exits:**
  - North → Flat Flats

### Shifting Sands (random, 0 to 6 h)

The desert hub east of Lopsided Lane. Its timer is **not fixed**. It varies with how many players move through, so a fresh regen can happen anywhere from immediately up to about 6 hours.

- **Timer:** random, 0 to 6 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Oil Fields
  - South → Queen's Quarry
  - East → Shrine
  - West → Lopsided Lane

### Oil Fields (15 min)

Dead-end fields north of the Shifting Sands.

- **Timer:** 15 min
- **Exits:**
  - South → Shifting Sands

### Queen's Quarry (2 h)

Mining quarry south of the Shifting Sands. From here you can climb **up to Queen's Lair** (a deeper area).

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - North → Shifting Sands
  - Up → Queen's Lair

### Shrine (30 min)

A roadside shrine east of the Shifting Sands. Connects north to the Temple.

- **Timer:** 30 min
- **Exits:**
  - West → Shifting Sands
  - North → Temple

### Temple (2 h)

Temple at the eastern edge of the desert.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - South → Shrine

## East path: Brown Road

The east exit opens toward the tavern district and, eventually, Rankle River.

### Brown Road (1 min)

Short, fast-regenerating corridor that fans out toward the Event Arena, the Tavern and East-shire.

- **Timer:** 1 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → Event Arena
  - South → Tavern
  - East → East-shire
  - West → Town Hall

### Event Arena (2 h)

Dead-end arena north of Brown Road, used for events.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - South → Brown Road

### Tavern (1 min)

The tavern. Very short regen, a good spot for quick passes. A trapdoor goes **down to the Basement**.

- **Timer:** 1 min
- **Exits:**
  - North → Brown Road
  - Down → Basement

### Basement (2 h)

Below the Tavern. From here you can descend further **down to the Labyrinth** (deeper area, not covered by this article).

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - Up → Tavern
  - Down → Labyrinth

### East-shire (6 h)

The far-east corridor. From East-shire you can leave Ratropia and enter the **Rankle River map**. See the dedicated Rankle River article for those rooms.

- **Timer:** 6 h
- **Exits:**
  - West → Brown Road
  - East → Rankle River (different map)
  - Down → Barrows-end (different area)

---

## West path: West-shire

The west exit is the gateway to the Hideous Dungeon.

### West-shire (1 h)

Western corridor leading to the Hideous Path.

- **Timer:** 1 h
- **Exits:**
  - East → Town Hall
  - West → Hideous Path

### Hideous Path (10 min)

The last room of Ratropia going west. Beyond it lies the **Hideous Dungeon map**. See the dedicated Hideous Dungeon article for everything past this point.

- **Timer:** 10 min
- **Exits:**
  - East → West-shire
  - West → Hideous Dungeon (different map)

---

## Vertical paths

Two short dead-end rooms accessed by going up or down from Town Hall.

### Up-shire (2 h)

Above Town Hall.

- **Timer:** 2 h
- **Exits:**
  - Down → Town Hall

### Down-shire (5 h)

Below Town Hall.

- **Timer:** 5 h
- **Exits:**
  - Up → Town Hall
