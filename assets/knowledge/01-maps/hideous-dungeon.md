# Hideous Dungeon

The Hideous Dungeon is the underground area west of Ratropia. You enter it through Hideous Path (the last room of Ratropia going west). This article walks the dungeon starting from **Hall of Scorn**, the central hub once you cross over from Ratropia.

For each room you'll find:

- A short description of what it is
- **Timer:** how long resources take to regenerate before the room can be looted again (`TBD` for now, will be filled later)
- **Exits:** every connected room and the direction or command to use

![Hideous Dungeon map](images/hideous-dungeon-map.jpg)

Rooms mapped by Fiodlander

A few conventions on the map:

- Thick dashed lines are the standard cardinal exits (north, south, east, west).
- Thin dashed lines mark **secret or alternate routes** between rooms.

For everything east of Hideous Path, see the **Ratropia** article.

## North path: Lover's Vault

The northern branch climbs through a vault and ends in the cliff country.

### Lover's Vault (TBD)

A high vault sitting above Imperious Falls. The map shows a `!d` marker, the command used to drop down into the falls.

- **Timer:** TBD
- **Exits:**
  - South to Hall of Scorn
  - Down (`!d`) to Imperious Falls

### Imperious Falls (TBD)

The waterfall basin below Lover's Vault. From here you can climb back up, or follow the cliffs west.

- **Timer:** TBD
- **Exits:**
  - Up to Lover's Vault
  - West to Cliff Track

### Cliff Track (TBD)

A narrow trail along the cliffs. The northern end leads up to Wayward Ward.

- **Timer:** TBD
- **Exits:**
  - East to Imperious Falls
  - North (or up) to Wayward Ward

### Wayward Ward (TBD)

## West path: Ardent Graveyard

A single, dense room that also serves as the head of several hidden paths shown as thin dashed lines on the map.

### Ardent Graveyard (TBD)

The graveyard west of Hall of Scorn. The thin dashed lines around it indicate **secret routes** linking it to the falls area to the north and to the chapel area to the south. These shortcuts are not standard cardinal exits; treat them as hidden paths to discover in-game.

- **Timer:** TBD
- **Exits:**

  - Hidden paths (thin dashed on the map) to the Imperious Falls area and to the Bygone Chapel area
